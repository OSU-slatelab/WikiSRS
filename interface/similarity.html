Judge similarity of two real-world people, places, or organizations

In this survey you will evaluate the similarity of 34 pairs of real-world people, places, or organizations, named in English. For example, you might see a pair like this one:

How similar are these two entities?
London Paris

For each pair you are shown, we ask you to assign a similarity score for the two people, places, or organizations. You give your answer on a sliding scale from "Not at all similar" to "Extremely similar".

To make sure you are paying attention, we have included some questions for which we know the right answer. If you read the questions carefully, you will definitely get those right.

The survey should take about 5 minutes and you will be paid $0.75 for participating.

At the end of the survey, we will ask you some short questions about you and your language background. You will be paid no matter what answers you give to those questions, so please answer truthfully.
